Overview



This Android app allows one to order food from mock restaurants, track orders, receive notifications, and re-order past orders. The app is much akin to GrubHub.

Each order is saved on an internal database which is manipulated through the Room API. Meanwhile, Google Maps is used to display the location of each restaurant.
