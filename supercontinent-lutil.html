Overview

Online Multiplayer Exploration Game



Supercontinent Lútil is a full-stack game that works entirely in the web browser. The game puts online multiplayer first, so every action must be sent to the back-end server and echoed to the other players. Players can walk around a vast, procedurally-generated world and fight monsters to grow stronger.

For this project, it's important to keep not only extensibility and maintainability in mind but also performance. Since player and enemy positions must be updated many times per second, I had to develop a server that would keep up.

Thus far, React Hooks have been the best part of the project! I realized that taking an hour or two to learn new technology can sometimes have monumental impact. I explain briefly in the video what React Hooks are.
